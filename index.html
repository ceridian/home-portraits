Home Portraits

By SL Ballew


Find Out More

As a realtor you get to experience the joy of a young couple buying their first home or the melancholy of watching a family closing down and selling a family estate.

How do you, as a realtor, thank your clients for putting their faith and trust in you to see them through one of the most important decisions of their lives? Home Portraits by SL Ballew. A perfect gift that keeps on giving.

For that young couple buying their first home, a treasured reminder of where it all began and for that family that is passing on their beloved home to a new family, a reminder of all the years of memories made there.

Each home portrait lovingly antiqued, custom matted, and framed with the clients name and special date. As an added bonus the the mat is hand painted to match the portrait and your company name and address is added to the back of the picture so that your client will always remember who helped them.
